Pasta Sauce Recipe

Description

An easy recipe to make a delightful and slightly spicy pasta (and pizza) sauce

Ingredients

  • 1KG of Roma Tomatoes
  • 100 Grams of Jalapenos
  • 3 (Mixed) Bell peppers
  • 200 Grams of Tomato Puree
  • 1 Teaspoon of Oregano

Steps

  1. Dice the Tomatoes, Jalapenos and Bell Peppers into small pieces
  2. Use a mixer to mix the diced ingredients until there are no solid pieces left
  3. Add the Tomato Puree and the teaspoon of Oregano
  4. Stir well until the Tomato Puree has fully mixed into the sauce and the oregano is properly spread out
  5. Now simmer the sauce on a low heat and stir occasionally until the sauce has reached the desired thickness
