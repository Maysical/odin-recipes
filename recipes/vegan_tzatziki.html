Vegan Tzatziki Recipe

Description

An easy recipe for a delightful vegan Tzatziki, with only 4 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 400 Grams of Oat Yoghurt
    • Can be substituted for another plant based Yoghurt
  • 8 Medium sized cloves of Garlic
  • 3/4th of a Cucumber
  • A teaspoon of Oregano

Steps

  1. Prepare Cucumber
    1. Peel the Cucumber
    2. Chop off the stem
    3. Grate (3/4th of) Cucumber
    4. Drain grated Cucumber to prevent getting a watery Tzatziki
  2. Prepare Garlic
    1. Peel garlic cloves
    2. Use garlic press on the peeled cloves
  3. Mix the grated Cucumber and pressed Garlic with the Oat Yoghurt
  4. Mix the Oregano into the Yoghurt
  5. Store in the fridge and for the best taste, wait for 24 hours before consuming
