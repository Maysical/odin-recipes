Vegan Pita Gyros Recipe

Description

An easy recipe to make delicious Vegan Pita Gyros

Ingredients

  • ~160 Grams of Vegan Chicken Pieces
    • My favorite are the Vivera Vegan Chicken Pieces
  • 1 Tablespoons of Gyros spices mix
    • I'll be using Verstegen Gyros Spice mix
  • Pita bread
  • Red onion
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Tzatziki
    • TIP: use the Tzatziki from my Vegan Tzatziki recipe

Steps

Vegan Gyros

  1. Bake the Vegan chicken pieces till they're ready for consumption
  2. Add water (usually about 1-2 cups) to the pan till all the pieces of water are below the surface of the water
  3. Add a tablespoon of the Gyros spice mix to the water and stir well
  4. Let the mixture of vegan pieces and the spice water simmer at a low heat till all the water has evaporated.
    • Make sure to stir occasionally

Prepare the pita

  1. While the mixture is simmering, spread tzatziki over the pita(s)
  2. Add Lettuce to the pita
  3. Add some slices of tomato
  4. Add some slices of red onion
  5. Once the vegan gyros has finished simmering, add the vegan gyros
  6. Roll up the pita and enjoy!
